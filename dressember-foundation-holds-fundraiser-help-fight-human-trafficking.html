 Posted on August 31, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  69 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

CHARLOTTE — Several well-known Charlotteans are teaming up to help fight human trafficking.

It’s the mission of The Dressember Foundation.

The nonprofit uses fashion to raise money and awareness about the fight against human trafficking.
