Gina Gershon Biography

Gina Gershon is an American actress. She is widely known for starring in Cocktail (1988), Red Heat (1988), Showgirls (1995), Bound (1996), Face/Off (1997), The Insider (1999), Demonlover (2002), P.S. I Love You (2007), Five Minarets in New York (2010), Killer Joe (2011) and House of Versace (2013). Gina also had supporting roles in FX’s Rescue Me and HBO’s How to Make It in America. She currently stars Gladys Jones on The CW teen drama series Riverdale.
