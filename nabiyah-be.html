Who is “Daisy Jones & The Six” Actor Nabiyah Be?

Nabiyah Be (born on August 15, 1992) is a 30-year-old Brazillian actress and musician who played the role of Linda in the 2018 Marvel production “Black Panther.” Many people recognize her as the daughter of the famous Jamaican singer Jimmy Cliff, who won a Grammy Award in 1985 for his album “Cliff Hanger.”

According to sources, Nabiyah has been working as a theatre artist since she was 7; in 2018, she won The BAM Award for her performance in “Black Panther” and became the first Black Brazilian female to win an American award for theatre work.

In 2023, Be entered the TV industry with the Netflix drama “Daisy Jones & The Six.” In this musical drama, which is based on the 70s band Fleetwood Mac, Nabiyah played Simone Jackson and worked alongside stars like Will Harrison, Josh Whitehouse, Riley Keough, and Sam Claflin.

What is Nabiyah Be’s Nationality?

On August 15, 1992, Nabiyah Be (age 30) was born to her father, Jimmy Cliff, and mother, Sônia Gomes, in Salvador, Brazil. According to sources, she has two siblings, Lilty and Aken Cliff. The actress was only 5 when she began accompanying her father on international music tours.

In 1999 she returned to Salvador and worked as a vocalist for exceptionally talented artists like Carlinhos Brown and Daniela Mercury; meanwhile, she also joined a local theatre company. Somewhere in her teen years, she moved to New York, America.

After coming to NY with the American dream, Nabiyah realized how tough it is to become an actor these days, but instead of getting overwhelmed by the competition, she performed at different theatres like Rockwood Music Hall and Apollo Theater and built a reputation in the theatre business.

How Did Nabiyah Become a Successful Actor?

After working years in the theatre industry, Nabiyah’s acting skills got polished, but she was not getting any chance to prove her talent.

Luckily in 2016, she auditioned for a Marvel movie where Sarah Finn recognized her talent and offered her the role of Linda in “Black Panther,” which came out two years later on February 16, 2018. The movie was a hit and won 114 awards, including 3 Oscar Awards.

Her next project became a short movie titled “White Wedding.” It was a Melody C. Roscher-directed film that was screened at Sundance Film Festival. Although Be stays more connected to acting, she never fails to spare some time for music.

These days she is working on her debut music album, which will come out very soon, and her first-ever TV project, “Daisy Jones & The Six,” is available to be streamed on Netflix.

Nabiyah has posted numerous traveling pics on her Instagram account, indicating how much she loves traveling. In June 2022, she visited Greece and shared some photos on her social media handles; one was with a handsome guy named Giacomo Rocchini.
